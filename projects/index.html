VR Simulation Workflow Design

Design of VR Simulations for Law Enforcement & Public Safety This project focuses on creating immersive virtual environments to train law enforcement officers and public safety personnel in high-stress decision-making. 🔍 Project Overview By integrating Criminology and Crime Science theories, I designed high-fidelity VR scenarios that replicate real-world legal and tactical challenges. I translated complex legal protocols into intuitive virtual workflows for effective situational training. Scenario Design: Developed the “Initial Investigation & Convenience Store Disturbance” VR scenario, focusing on legal compliance and officer safety. Behavioral Modeling: Incorporated criminal behavior patterns and victimology into the simulation to enhance environmental realism. Technical Specification: Authored technical requirements for VR developers to ensure the simulation maintains high procedural fidelity. 🛠 Key Contributions Academic Research: Leveraged findings from the Journal of SMART Policing to build data-backed virtual training modules. Subject Matter Expertise: Bridged the communication gap between law enforcement experts and VR engineers to ensure accurate protocol implementation. UX/UI for Training: Designed user interfaces within the VR space that allow for the collection of quantitative trainee response data.
